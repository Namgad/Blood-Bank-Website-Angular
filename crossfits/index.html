ABOUT US

The main aim of developing this system is to provide blood to the people who are in need of blood. The number of persons who are in need of blood are increasing in large number day by day. Using this system user can search blood group available in the city. In order to help people who are in need of blood, this Online Blood Bank management system can help them by getting the blood near them. Now they don"t need to visit every blood blank for requirement of blood.
